Some of My Favorite Activities for Kids

String Paint Art:

Dip string into paint to create

4th of July fireworks.

Fall Leaves:

Collect fall leaves and make a leaf collage.

Pumpkin Geoboard:

Place golf tees on a pumpkin and

use elastics for geometric shapes.

Earth Day Activity:

Mixing shaving cream with green and blue paint.

Sunflower Still Art:

Observe flowers and paint.

Caterpillar Paint Stamp:

Slice a pool noodle into stamps for paint.
